Mission Statement

Our mission at Faver is to raise awareness and educate all users of the food supply chain of the devastating effects of food waste. We firmly believe that if people come together and actively work towards preventing food loss we can significantly increase sustainability.

Our Users

Here at Faver we like to believe our website and its services targets everyone involved in the food supply chain from consumers to processing facilities. Meet some of our users...

Background: Martin has over 15 years of experience in the food distribution industry. He is highly knowledgeable in logistics and is dedicated to reducing waste in the supply chain.
Needs & Goals: Martin devoted considerable effort to creating data summaries and comprehensive guides on food supply for each product. He sought additional information regarding food waste data in other eateries as a result of government regulations. Furthermore, he is highly motivated to include further sustainable practices into the supply chain.

Background: Jenny is an executive chef at a restaurant that specializes in fine dining. Jenny is passionate about sustainability and is aware the destruction food loss has on the environment. She is determined to minimize food loss as much as possible in her kitchen.
Needs & Goals: Jenny requires a website that has data on different types of food loss and must be able to filter data according to her needs with various parameters. Her goal is to use the the advanced filters to make her own parameters and use the data to cut food waste down by 50%

Background: Mark is a high school teacher teaching environment science. He has a degree in environmental science and his education has taught him the drastic effect food waste can have on the environment. Mark wants to utilise a software solution to teach his students about food waste.
Needs & Goals: Mark requires a website with a large source of data to teach his students about sustainability, including interactive graphs and tables to visualize the data. His goal is to educate the younger generation about food waste and its consequences.
