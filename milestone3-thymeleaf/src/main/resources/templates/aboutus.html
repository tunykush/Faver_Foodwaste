About Us

Welcome to Faver. Our platform offers an impartial examination of the origins and consequences of food waste, from the point of harvest to the point of consumption.

Our Mission

To inform and empower users to understand and address food loss and waste, contributing to a more sustainable and equitable food system.

What We Do

  • Detailed statistics and analysis of food loss and waste.
  • Tools to identify and compare similar loss/waste patterns.

Our Team

A dedicated group of developers and researchers with a passion for sustainability and technology.

Why It Matters

Reducing food loss and waste helps combat climate change, enhance food security, and preserve resources.
